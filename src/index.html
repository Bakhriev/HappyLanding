Why Do Families Choose Our Pharmacy?

We care about the well-being of your family. Our customers choose Happy Family Pharmacy for the following reasons:

  • Professional approach. We employ professional pharmacists who regularly improve their skills. Thus, you can always count on knowledgeable assistance.
  • Affordable prices. The affordability of our products is due to the absence of intermediaries, direct deliveries from manufacturers, and a professional approach to operations. In addition, by purchasing medications for a long course of treatment, you can save a decent amount.
  • Quality assurance. It is very important for us to create an effective system for monitoring the quality of medicinal products from the moment they are available until they are released to the customers. Moreover, all products presented in our pharmacy are certified and comply with the current standards and requirements.
  • Wide range of products. We want to meet the needs of every family member, so we try to have a great variety of medicines and medical products.
  • Timely delivery. You may always rely on us to deliver your prescriptions on time. We check every package before sending it to you.
  • Individual approach to each client. We pay maximum attention to each patient, taking into account individual needs and desires.
  • Confidentiality. We preserve medical confidentiality and respect patient privacy

An additional positive aspect of a chance to order medications online allows our customers to sit at home in a comfortable environment and add the necessary goods to their basket.

Our team approached the design of our website with all responsibility, ensuring that it fully meets the requirements of clients. You can order medications in just a few clicks. You just need to go to the Happy Family Store website, find the required medicinal product in the search menu or the corresponding section, add it to the cart, and place an order by filling out the required information fields. Your order will be delivered in no time

Online Refills & Home Delivery

We give our customers the ability to conveniently refill their prescriptions online and have them delivered to their doorstep. You can have your prescription refill sent to us electronically and we will take care of the rest. Our goal is to accurately complete every refill as soon as possible because we know your time and your health are valuable. We won’t keep you waiting. When dispensing a medicine, our employees explain in detail how and when to take it, what to take with it, what dosage you should adhere to, and how to store the medicine at home.

Signature Medication Packs

The mission of the Happy Family Pharmacy is high-quality service and satisfaction of the population’s needs when it comes to the treatment, prevention of various diseases, and maintenance of health. Pharmacists are here to provide you and your family with care and make your medication intake as simple as possible.

Our pharmacy can curate individual medication bundles for each family member. This means that you will not have to worry that you or any other family member missed pill intake or took the wrong dosage. Everyone will have all the pills that they need to take bundled together for the day. Moreover, our pharmacists will also divide daily pill intake between morning, noon, and evening. Even if you have complex treatment plans, our signature medication packs will make it as simple as possible.

Family Events

At HFS Pharmacy, we want to be much more than just a pharmacy where people can purchase quality medication. Since we are a part of our community, we want to do everything in our power to support it. We treat every customer as a family member and have many events that allow our customers to take better care of their health and much more.

Educational Courses

In order to help patients better understand their medical issues and how to take care of themselves, we provide a wide range of educational courses. We organize free health programs every month where members of the community can learn more about their health conditions.

Blood Drive

One of the ways to strengthen a community is to hold blood donation events. We hold blood drive events during which not only our employees can donate blood and help save someone’s life, but also the members of our community. Any healthy person over 18 years of age can be a blood donor.

Other Events

We also add other events to your calendar when a good reason arises. It can be a new medical product launch or engaging conferences or webinars.

We also encourage our employees to share their time and skills in memberships and community events.

Stay Connected

If you haven’t used one of our many services, call or visit us today! Thanks to the high qualifications of our pharmacists, you can consult and ask them questions regarding any product presented on our website. We love and know our work and are ready to help those who need our care and support!
